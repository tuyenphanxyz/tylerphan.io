Overview

Managing projects can be complex, but Coca simplifies the process with a structured yet flexible approach. Built with a user-friendly design, it allows teams to plan, track, and execute projects efficiently. With real-time collaboration, automation features, and insightful analytics, Coca ensures that every project stays on track and meets its deadlines.

Key Features

Coca adapts to your workflow, making project management more productive and engaging.

  • Task Management – Create, assign, and prioritize tasks effortlessly.
  • Kanban & List Views – Organize projects with flexible task visualization options.
  • Team Collaboration – Comment, tag teammates, and keep discussions within tasks.
  • Progress Tracking – Monitor project status with real-time updates and analytics.
  • File Sharing & Attachments – Upload documents, images, and files directly to tasks.

Design

Components

Coca offers a structured UI system with well-organized components, including:

  • Dashboard – A centralized hub to get an overview of all projects.
  • Task Manager – An intuitive space to create, edit, and organize tasks.
  • Calendar & Timeline – Visualize project timelines and deadlines.
  • Team Chat – Built-in messaging for quick and efficient team communication.
  • Analytics & Reports – Generate insights with detailed charts and performance metrics.
