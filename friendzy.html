Overview

Friendzy – Dating App is a modern dating platform designed to help users easily connect, find friends, and match with like-minded individuals. With a clean, youthful, and intuitive UI, Friendzy offers a seamless dating experience.

Problem Statement

This project focused on building a user-friendly, secure, and high-performance dating app, ensuring smooth interactions and meaningful connections.

Branding

The branding is modern, playful, and romantic. The logo is minimalistic, combining a heart symbol with a subtle chat bubble to represent meaningful connections. The design embraces a warm and inviting color palette, paired with smooth typography and a sleek, trendy illustration style to create a fun yet sophisticated dating experience.

Onboarding

Create your profile, share your interests, and meet like-minded people. Your journey to meaningful friendships and romance starts here!"

Login & Create Account

The Login & Create Account flow in Friendzy is designed to be seamless and user-friendly, ensuring quick access while maintaining security.This flow ensures that users can quickly start exploring Friendzy while keeping their data secure and their experience smooth.

Discover

The Discover feature in Friendzy is designed to help users find the most compatible matches with ease. It includes smart filters and a nearby search to enhance the dating experience. The Discover flow ensures users find meaningful connections quickly, whether through refined filters or exploring matches nearby.

Matches

The Matching feature in Friendzy is designed to create meaningful connections when two users show mutual interest. It ensures an engaging and seamless experience for users looking for friendships or romance.

Messages

The Messaging feature in Friendzy allows users to build deeper connections through seamless and engaging conversations.With Friendzy’s messaging, conversations go beyond text, making every interaction more authentic and exciting!
